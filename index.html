About Ghomi Marvels

Ghomi Marvels Church, located in New Kahawa Sukari, Nairobi, is the ministry of Bishop Joshua and Emily Mulinge. Committed to preaching God's Kingdom, Ghomi Marvels Church strives to uplift and guide its congregation in faith and spiritual growth.

Under the leadership of Bishop Joshua and Emily, the ministry also flourishes with Pastor Shalom Koroge serving as the lead pastor. The church community is focused on delivering the message of Christ while fostering a deep connection with God through worship, teaching, and service.

Meet Our Leaders

Bishop Joshua & Bishop Emily Mulinge

Bishop Joshua and Bishop Emily Mulinge have been faithful servants of the Lord, leading GHOMI Church with unwavering dedication and a deep commitment to God's Kingdom. Their ministry is rooted in the teachings of Christ, and they have tirelessly worked to shepherd their congregation, fostering spiritual growth and community through the power of God's Word.

Through their leadership, GHOMI Church has become a beacon of faith, compassion, and hope, guiding believers to live out their Christian walk with purpose and conviction. Their impact in ministry reflects a heart for service, discipleship, and the advancement of God's work on earth.

Lead Pastor Shalom Koronge

Pastor Shalom Koroge has been a tremendous blessing to GHOMI Church, leading with grace, wisdom, and a heart devoted to Christ. His leadership is marked by a deep commitment to the spiritual well-being of the congregation, consistently motivating and inspiring us to grow in our faith and live out our Christian calling.

Pastor Shalom's passion for God's Word and his ability to connect with people on a personal level have made him a driving force in nurturing a strong and united church family. Under his guidance, GHOMI Church continues to thrive, grounded in the love of Christ and empowered by the Holy Spirit to fulfill God's purpose.
